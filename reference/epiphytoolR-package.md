# epiphytoolR: Epiphytotic Modeling Tools

Provides common base-level functions for undertaking epiphytotic
modelling and simulation studies in R. Included are tools for modelling
the spatial movement of splash and wind dispersed plant disease
inoculum, estimating disease progress, and formatting and imputing
weather data for use in process-based plant disease models.
